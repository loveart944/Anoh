About Attendance Calendar

Key Features

  • **Attendance Tracking:** Mark days as Present, Absent, Holiday, Half Day, Leave, Emergency, Sick, or Festival.
  • **Daily Notes:** Add custom notes for any specific day on the calendar.
  • **Overtime Management:** Record overtime hours for individual days.
  • **Shift Scheduling:** Assign various shift types (Morning, Afternoon, Evening, Night, General, etc.) to days.
  • **Monthly Summary:** Get a detailed statistical summary of your attendance for the current month.
  • **Multiple Calendars:** Create and manage separate attendance calendars for different purposes (e.g., personal, work).
  • **Birthday Reminders:** Store and manage birthdays of friends, family, and colleagues.
  • **Personal Profile:** Customize your profile with your name and email.
  • **Local Data Storage:** All your data is securely saved on your device for persistence.
